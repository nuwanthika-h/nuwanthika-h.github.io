Research Experience

Undergraduate Research Project

Title: A Molecular Phylogenetic and Comparative Morpho-Anatomical Study on Some Selected Madhuca spp. (Sapotaceae) in Sri Lanka

Description: This study addressed the research gap in the morphology, anatomy, and molecular characterization of the genus *Madhuca* in Sri Lanka. The work involved optimizing the CTAB method for DNA extraction, developing PCR protocols to amplify the nuclear ITS1 region, and using bioinformatics tools such as BioEdit, BLAST, and MEGA for sequence analysis, alignment, and phylogenetic tree construction.
