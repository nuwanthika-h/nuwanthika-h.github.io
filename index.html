Research Experience

Undergraduate Research Project

Title: A Molecular Phylogenetic and Comparative Morpho-Anatomical Study on Some Selected Madhuca spp. (Sapotaceae) in Sri Lanka

Duration: August 2023 - April 2024

Description: This study addressed the research gap in the morphology, anatomy, and molecular characterization of the genus Madhuca in Sri Lanka, which currently requires floral characteristics for typification. The work involved optimizing the CTAB method for DNA extraction and developing PCR protocols to amplify the nuclear ITS1 region. Bioinformatics tools such as BioEdit and MEGA were used for sequence analysis, alignment, and phylogenetic tree construction.
